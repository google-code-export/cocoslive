cocos Live Shutdown FAQs

Why are we shutting down cocos Live?

As much as we have loved providing you this service, we are shutting down to move on to new ventures.

What will happen to this service?

We will officially shutdown this service on December 1, 2011 but in the interim, you can expect the same support and service as you have now.

Can we add new games to cocos Live?

Unfortunately not. Since we are now transitioning, we recommend you create your own instance of cocos Live in Google App Engine, or use an alternative service like Game Center or OpenFeint.

What will happen to the games we have that use this site?

Data from games that use this site will no longer be available as of December 1, 2011. However, we will provide a tool that will let you download the scores in the near future.

Who can I talk to if I have questions about the impending product shutdown?

You can contact us at info@sapusmedia.com and we will get back to you promptly.
